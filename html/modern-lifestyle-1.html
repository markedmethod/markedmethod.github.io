Ergonomic Grip and Lighter Holder


A client produced a problem that had not yet been solved: ensure proper grip on glasswares used in the burgeoning medical industry.

The features desired for this product are:

  • Universal Fit
  • Comfortable Grip
  • Lighter Stowing
  • Customizable

The resulting design is a cost-effective and scalable approach to take to market.

The final design was conceived after several iterations were prototyped. The system only requires small modifications to produce a grip or clip element made out of natural materials like leather and/or stainless steel. Going into limited production and testing the market is the next step.
