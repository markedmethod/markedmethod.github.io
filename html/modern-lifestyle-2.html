Modern Lifestyle 2
 
 

Ergonomic Magnifier Glass and Lighter Holder


A client produced a problem that had not yet been solved: a combined fire-starting tool with a backup source. The target application is a demographic of medical users who do not wish to inhale butane gas from the use of a lighter, but in desperate situations would be willing to use.

The features desired for this product are:

  • Comfortable Grip
  • Balanced
  • Mini-Bic Lighter Stowing
  • Simple & Non-technical appeal

The resulting design is a cost-effective and scalable approach to take to market.

The final design was conceived after several iterations were prototyped. Going into limited production and testing the market is the next step.
